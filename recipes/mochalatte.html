Mocha Latte

Description

Chocolate that wakes you up.

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Syrup

Steps

  1. Brew coffee using a method of your choosing.
  2. Fill up a coffee cup halfway with milk. Put milk in microwave and run the microwave for one minute.
  3. Using a towel to prevent burning your hands, remove the coffee cup from the microwave. Add four spoonfuls of chocolate syrup to the cup and stir until fully mixed.
  4. With the brewed coffee, fill the coffee cup to the top.
