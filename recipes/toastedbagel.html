Toasted Bagel

Description

Crunchy, sweet, and carby.

Ingredients

  • Bagel Top
  • Bagel Bottom
  • Spread (optional)

Steps

  1. Place bagel top in one side of toaster.
  2. Place bagel bottom in other side of toaster.
  3. Pull down lever, activating the toaster.
  4. Catch bagel top and bottoms when they escape from the toaster, being careful as to not burn yourself.
  5. Add desired spread.
