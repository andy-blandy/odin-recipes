Scrambled Eggs

Description

Yellow clouds cooked in warm butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Set stovetop to med-high, and put a pan on stove.
  2. Place a slab of butter in the middle of the pan. As it begins to melt, swirl the butter around to cover every inch of the pan
  3. Once all of the butter has melted, crack both eggs and add them to the pan.
  4. Grab a spatula and quickly scramble the eggs. Continue scrambling until the eggs are fully cooked to tase. Remove pan from heat when necessary to prevent burning.
  5. Add any desired spices or toppings.
